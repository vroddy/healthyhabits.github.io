Routine 1: Cardio and Strength Training

        Warm-up (5 minutes):
        
        Jogging or jumping jacks to increase heart rate and warm up the body.
        Cardiovascular Exercise (20 minutes):
        
        Option 1: Running or brisk walking on a treadmill or outdoors.
        Option 2: Cycling on a stationary bike or outdoors.
        Option 3: High-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout, alternating between intense bursts of exercise (e.g., jumping jacks, burpees, mountain climbers) and short rest periods.
        Strength Training (25 minutes):
        
        Perform a circuit of exercises targeting major muscle groups.
        Examples: Squats, lunges, push-ups, dumbbell rows, shoulder presses, planks.
        Perform each exercise for a set number of repetitions or time and move to the next exercise without rest.
        Repeat the circuit for the allotted time.
        Cool-down and Stretching (10 minutes):
        
        Perform light stretching exercises to improve flexibility and cool down the body.
