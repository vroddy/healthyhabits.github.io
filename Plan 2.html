Routine 2: Yoga and Core Workout

        Warm-up (5 minutes):
        
        Gentle stretching exercises to warm up the muscles.
        Yoga Practice (30 minutes):
        
        Follow a guided yoga routine or flow that includes a combination of poses, stretches, and breathing exercises.
        Focus on improving flexibility, balance, and mindfulness.
        Core Workout (20 minutes):
        
        Perform a series of exercises targeting the abdominal and back muscles.
        Examples: Planks, crunches, Russian twists, bicycle crunches, leg raises, supermans.
        Perform each exercise for a set number of repetitions or time and take short breaks as needed.
        Cool-down and Stretching (5 minutes):
        
        Finish with gentle stretches, focusing on the core and other muscle groups worked during the routine.
